Community Launches HARD.fun Memecoin Launchpad

Nov 20, 2024
2 mins

The HARD community is excited to introduce the launch of HARD.fun, the first decentralized memecoin platform. Unlike centralized launchpads like pump.fun and Moonshot, HARD.fun goes back to crypto first principles by distributing governance and rewards back to the community.

HARDcore Decentralization

Web3’s superpower is using decentralization to empower and engage active participants by giving them ‘skin in the game’. HARD.fun is not just another memecoin launchpad—it’s a community-led initiative where HARD token holders can directly influence its destiny – and their own. A portion of all HARD.fun protocol fees are automatically sent to a community wallet, enabling hodlers to vote on reward allocations and platform changes. HARD holders can vote on critical parameters like:

  • Deploy Service Fee: Sets entry cost for new tokens, and manages network usage and spam.
  • Trading Fee: Gets applied to token trades, and balances liquidity and transaction fairness.
  • Migration Fee: Sets the cost for migrating tokens that reach the bonding curve threshold to an onchain DEX.
  • Liquidity for DEX: Sets the liquidity required on the bonding curve for a token to be migrated to an onchain DEX.

By voting on these parameters, HARD token holders gain significant influence over both immediate operations and long-term direction of HARD.fun’s vison and the value it creates.

3 Reasons HARD.fun Wins

  1. It’s Simple

Deploying memecoins should be easy. HARD.fun leverages KavaAI, allowing anyone to launch tokens without advanced coding skills. With just a few clicks on a user-friendly chatbot, set your parameters and bring your memecoin ideas to life. The only limit to success is your imagination and cult-making abilities.

  1. It’s Cross-Chain

HARD.fun’s cross-chain feature is powered by Kava Chain’s bridge architecture, allowing memecoins to operate seamlessly across ecosystems like Ethereum, Cosmos IBC, and eventually Solana, Tron, and BNB Chain. This interoperability expands the potential reach and trading opportunities across your memecoins across various platforms, including Telegram’s vast network.

  1. It’s Community-Led

HARD.fun not only brings utility and rewards to HARD token holders but also goes back to first-principles distribution control and ownership directly to you, the community. This community-first reward-sharing model and governance cycle mean long-term growth and ongoing participation for all HARD holders.

So, if you’ve got the creativity, sense of humor, and community spirit, HARD.fun is your memecoin launchpad. Grab the hot new Telegram sticker pack, say gm to the gang of HARD holders on Telegram, and follow HARD.fun on X for alpha on how to get the most out of the platform. 

Welcome to HARD.fun - the only memecoin platform designed to start–and end with the community!
