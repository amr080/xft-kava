Introducing: The HARD.fun Proposal

Nov 4, 2024
6 mins

The HARD community is thrilled to showcase an industry first with the proposed launch of the Hard.fun memecoin platform. 

The rise of centralized memecoin launchpads like pump.fun, Moonshot, and gra.fun have shown that memecoins can be a powerful - and fun - platform. But these early memecoin launchpads are ultimately controlled and owned by their developers. 

‍

Introducing HARD.fun, the world’s first memecoin launchpad to embrace the essence of crypto by distributing governance and rewards back to the community that runs it. HARD.fun is the decentralized evolution of legacy memecoin launchpads, built on grassroots crypto values.

If It’s Not Decentralized, What’s The Point?

If Web3 has taught us anything, it's that if we want a decentralized future, we have to build it, and then give it away to communities to own, love, and bring to life. Let’s be honest, the world does not need yet another centralized memecoin launchpad. 

A portion of HARD.fun's protocol fees will be programmatically allocated to a community wallet address. From there, the HARD community can vote on where those rewards will go and how they will be used. That means you, anon. If you hold HARD tokens, you directly influence what HARD.fun becomes and how it deploys its rewards. HARD holders get to steer the direction of HARD.fun in the short-term, including voting on critical token parameters including:

  • Deploy Service Fee: Sets entry cost for new tokens, and manages network usage and spam.
  • Trading Fee: Applied to token trades, balances liquidity, and transaction fairness.
  • Migration Fee: Sets the cost of migrating tokens that reach the bonding cure threshold to onchain DEX.
  • Liquidity for DEX: Liquidity required on the HARD.fun bonding curve for a token to be migrated to an onchain DEX.

Holders can also guide the long-term evolution, by voting for large-scale upgrades in the future (e.g. HARD.fun V2). The sum of these governance decisions gives the HARD community more skin in the game than any other memecoin launchpad and creates a sustainable value loop for long-term growth.

Creating Memecoins Is Not HARD Anymore

Memecoins succeed by the virality, adoption, and community energy that they generate - that’s hard enough, deploying a memecoin shouldn’t be. Hard.fun will leverage KavaAI to bring your memetic visions to life, and iteration is the name of the game. A few clicks on HARD.fun’s user-friendly token launch chatbot lets creators easily design coins and set the parameters without in-depth technological knowledge.

Table stakes for launching a memecoin should be your good ideas, not your ability to code, so HARD.fun removes technical-knowledge as a barrier to entry and opens up the potential user base so wide that even your aunt could launch a coin. Creators, traders, and everyone in between will only be limited by what their imagination.

The reward-sharing model for user-generated coins and community governance voting on distribution further enhance the community-first approach by empowering active creators to stick around after the release of their project.

Cross-Chain Memecoins for Everyone

One of the most exciting features of the HARD.fun platform is the chance to go cross-chain with your memecoins. Kava Chain’s interoperable bridge architecture, users are not restricted to only the Kava ecosystem.

Every memecoin created on HARD.fun may be able to seamlessly transit across Kava EVM, Cosmos IBC, and Ethereum ecosystems, and eventually, across Solana, Tron, BNB Chain, and beyond. HARD.fun is bringing memecoins to the world, the integration with the Tron ecosystem alone will enable easy transactions through the Telegram app and access to its 950 million global user base. This means memecoins launched on HARD.fun can be traded on other chains, and users on other chains will be able to trade memecoins on HARD.fun.

The launch of Hard.fun will also renew the focus and build new utility for the HARD token holders both old and new. HARD.fun’s reward structure for rewarding HARD holders also creates long-term benefits in addition to the short-term speculative excitement of the HARD token itself. A virtuous cycle of community rewards and governance attracts a larger community over time, increasing the transaction volume and the underlying value of the HARD token itself.

‍

The Future of Memecoins Is HARD.fun

So, if you’ve got a great sense of humor, an obsessive creative streak, or are moved by the good ol’ fashioned community spirit, HARD.fun is waiting for you. It’s about time memecoin launchpads start and end with the community. 

HARD.fun is the only memecoin launchpad designed specifically to reward its users and leverage cutting-edge AI chatbots to eliminate friction. Good luck, and welcome to HARD.fun
